About Us

Local Guides. Real Adventure.

We are a local adventure tourism team based in Meghalaya, dedicated to providing safe, professional and unforgettable experiences in the hidden wonders of Krem Chympe and Khaddum Falls.

Our certified local guides lead every rafting trip, cave expedition and off-road journey — sharing generations of local knowledge so you can explore with confidence, respect for nature, and zero worries.

Eco Friendly

We follow eco-friendly, low-impact practices in every trip we run — protecting the caves, rivers and forests of Krem Chympe so they stay wild for the generations after us.

Our Mission

To make Krem Chympe one of Meghalaya's premier adventure destinations — delivering professional guiding, sustainable tourism, and memories that last a lifetime.
